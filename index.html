To enroll in our pilot training program, you need to be at least 17 years old, have completed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics, possess a valid medical certificate, and demonstrate proficiency in English. Additional requirements may apply for specific license types.

Training duration varies by program: PPL (3-6 months), CPL (12-18 months), ATPL (18-24 months). Aircraft maintenance programs typically take 2-3 years, while cabin crew training is completed in 3-6 months.
