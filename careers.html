Career Opportunities

Explore exciting career paths in the aviation industry

Commercial Pilot

Fly commercial aircraft for airlines and charter companies worldwide.

  • Average Salary: ₹15-50 Lakhs/year
  • Global opportunities
  • Career progression to Captain
  • Excellent benefits package

Aircraft Maintenance Engineer

Ensure aircraft safety through expert maintenance and inspection.

  • Average Salary: ₹8-25 Lakhs/year
  • High job security
  • Technical expertise valued
  • International certifications

Cabin Crew

Provide exceptional service and ensure passenger safety on flights.

  • Average Salary: ₹3-12 Lakhs/year
  • Travel opportunities
  • Customer service focus
  • Career growth to senior positions

Air Traffic Controller

Manage aircraft movements and ensure safe air traffic operations.

  • Average Salary: ₹10-30 Lakhs/year
  • Government positions available
  • High responsibility role
  • Excellent job security

Ground Operations

Handle airport operations, cargo, and passenger services.

  • Average Salary: ₹3-15 Lakhs/year
  • Multiple specializations
  • Entry-level opportunities
  • Career advancement paths

Aviation Management

Lead aviation businesses and manage airline operations.

  • Average Salary: ₹12-40 Lakhs/year
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Strategic decision making
  • Executive career path
